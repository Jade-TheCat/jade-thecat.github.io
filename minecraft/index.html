Below are the mods I currently have out or am working on, along with the Minecraft versions they support. I am currently working on Fabric ports for the 1.14.4 update.

I will not be updating my mods to 1.13.2.

Minecraft versions are marked as follows:
LEG (Legacy) means it won't get any new updates.
LTS (Long-Term Support) means it won't get new features, but will get bug fixes.
LS (Limited Support) means it will be getting limited new features.
AS (Active Support) means it will be getting all new features. UP (Upcoming) means the release is pending or WIP.
